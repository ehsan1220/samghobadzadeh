Sam Ghobadzadeh

Sam Ghobadzadeh

Biography:

I started painting seriously in 2000, in 2001 I pursued painting more professionally when I entered the university of art, and by attending the classes of Professor Abbas Inanlu and a short course of Professor Morteza Katouzian, I took the style of realism and photorealism.
But my interest in conceptual styles from the very beginning made me tend to my favorite style, Surrealism, and I created works in this style. I held my first important exhibition in Tehran in 2006 at Soreh University of Soreh Art and until today I have held many exhibitions in Iran and several European countries. One of the most important events in the field of painting for me was participating in the American World Championships in 2015 and 2014, in which I became a finalist. This made me enter European exhibitions. Currently, I continue my professional activity in the Netherlands.
